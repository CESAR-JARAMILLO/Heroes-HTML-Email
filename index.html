VRC TEAM SPOTLIGHT
THE YOUNGREN DIFFERENCE
Scott Youngren and his company, Youngren Construction, have been actively involved in building Homes for Our Troops since 2010. They consider it an honor to support veterans who have bravely served their country and are passionate about this cause. For that reason, The Heroes Project has partnered with Youngren Construction to build the Veteran Retreat Center.
Youngren Construction is committed to delivering exceptional service that surpasses clients' expectations. They possess the capacity to manage projects of varying scales and consistently aim to provide unparalleled support. With a wealth of experience spanning over three decades, they have earned a reputation for their unwavering dedication to excellence, dependability, and client satisfaction. We are proud to be partnering with Youngren Construction and are excited to work with them on this significant project.
https://youngrenconstruction.com/
